Blood Donation

Our school organized a blood donation drive in collaboration with the local blood bank. We invited trained professionals to set up a donation camp on our school premises and encouragedstudents and staff to participate.

Tree Plantation

Our school'senvironment club organized several tree plantation drives in and around the city. The students, along with the club coordinators, identified various locations that required more greenery and worked in collaboration with local authorities to obtain the necessary permissions.

Empowering Acid Attack Survivors

Our school's social service club collaborated with a local NGO that works for the rehabilitation and empowerment of acid attack survivors. We organized several fundraising events, including a charity walk and a bake sale, to raise money for the NGO.
